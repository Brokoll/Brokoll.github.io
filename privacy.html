Privacy Policy

Emotion-Focused Speaking Coaching (the “App”) respects users’ privacy and complies with applicable data protection laws, including GDPR.

1. Purpose of Data Collection

  • User registration and login management
  • Providing speaking coaching services and personalized feedback
  • Analysis of practice videos and audio recordings
  • Subscription and payment management
  • Customer support and service notifications

2. Data Collected

  • Email address
  • Nickname
  • Gender
  • Resume / self-introduction summary
  • Desired job position
  • Practice videos
  • Audio recordings

Login methods: Google Sign-In, Apple Sign-In, Email & Password

Automatically collected data: Device information, app usage logs, error logs

3. Data Retention

Personal data is retained only as long as necessary to fulfill its purpose, unless retention is required by law.

4. Data Sharing

The App does not sell or share personal data with third parties, except when required by law.

5. Data Processing Partners

  • RevenueCat – Subscription and payment processing
  • Google, Apple – Authentication services

6. User Rights

Users may request access, correction, deletion, or restriction of their personal data at any time.

7. International Users

The App may be used by users outside of South Korea. EU users have rights under GDPR. California residents may request deletion of personal data. The App does not sell personal data.
